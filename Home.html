About us:

MY ONSLAUGHT STARTS HERE (M.O.S.H.) amassed on June 16th, 2008 in the Chicagoland area. Started by classically trained musicians and brothers Filip and Mark, MY ONSLAUGHT STARTS HERE incorporates clean melodic vocals with screams and modern heavy metal.

The band is currently working on their EP album, which has no verified release date.
